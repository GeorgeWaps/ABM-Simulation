     In this blog, I will discuss the observation from the age-compartment model with rescaling function completely based on the policy, The study Utilized Agent Based Modeling(ABM) using Netlogo to simulate the spread of Covid-19 virus, based on the research data from Washington, USA. In this method the rate of change of total infection. this include the age-specific relaxation polity. Opening school/work and vaccine distribution.

        The Objective of this simulation is to used the ABM to simulate the spread of Covid-19 using age-structure SIR model. 


About the Case Study

The study by Vishaal Ram et .al. titled "ABM age-structure SIR model for COVID-19" a case study used a compartment model with scale function of population age-distributions.

An case study base on age-compartment model with a rescaling function completely based on the policy, where the intensity of the social distancing is proportional to the ICU occupancy and decreasing peak infection count. The scale function of population age-distribution is directly correlated with increasing peak ICU occupancy and decreasing peak infection count. 

 The immunity threshold is unaffected by the change in population parameters with the same proportion of the population being infected. Age-distributions, relaxing school and work restrictions has the effect of infecting the same proportion of the population across a smaller time frame.

What is Agent Based Modeling?

Agent-based model are computational simulation models that involve many discrete agents. it is usually implemented by simulation models in a computer, where each agent's behavioral rules are described in a algorithmic fashion. This allow modelers to implement complex internal properties of agent and the nontrivial behavioral rules. (info@libretext.org, 2020).

What is NetLogo?

Netlogo is programmable modeling environment which capable of simulating complex situations such as natural and social phenomena. (igi-global.com)

Netlogo is a modeling environment designed for coding and running agent-based simulation. it can accommodate reasonably large and complex simulation, and its execution speed is more than acceptable for most purposed. (info@libratext.org,Bookshelves, 2021)

Explanation of Test Setting

In this section, the main objective of this model is to investigate the effect of a population’s age distribution on the transmission and spread of a virus like COVID-19, which is both highly contagious and largely age-specific in its effect on the population.

The SIR model is a simple model for infectious disease in which the population is divided into three compartments: those susceptible to the disease, those infected with the disease, and those removed from the disease either through death or recovery or die.
